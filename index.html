About Me

Designing learning that sticks

I am a secondary math teacher turned EdTech founder. Spending over five years in the classroom really opened my eyes to a recurring challenge in education: we have no real visibility into student thinking.

So I built EdCipher Math. An AI-powered platform centered on the CAT (Cognitive Accountability Triangle). Students capture their reasoning. Teachers diagnose misconceptions. Parents get plain-language insight they can act on. Same data, three views, one feedback loop that hasn't existed in a single platform until now.

Currently focused on TSIA2 Math college placement prep in Texas, EdCipher is designed to plug into what teachers already use and add the one layer that's always been missing: the why behind every wrong answer.

The classroom experience that shaped this platform is the same one driving it forward.
